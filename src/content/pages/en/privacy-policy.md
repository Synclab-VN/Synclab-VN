## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Synclab handles information related to this website and our contact channels.

This website does not require visitors to create an account. Most information is provided only when a visitor chooses to contact us.

## Information We May Collect

We may receive your name, email address, company name, phone number, project details, support request, or other information you voluntarily provide.

We may also receive basic technical information from normal website hosting and security logs.

## How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, provide support, discuss services, improve our website, and protect our systems.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Synclab does not currently require account cookies for this website. If analytics, advertising pixels, or other tracking technologies are added later, this policy will be updated.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only with service providers or partners when needed to respond to your request, operate the website, comply with law, or protect legitimate interests.

## Data Retention and Security

We keep information only for as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described above. We use reasonable safeguards, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## User Rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have provided, subject to legal and operational requirements.

## Third-party Links

This website may link to third-party services such as Google Maps, Gmail, or Zalo. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page.

## Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact syncl4p+support-privacy@gmail.com.
